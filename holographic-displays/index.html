Glasscape Technology

Pepper's Ghost Technology

Our advanced implementation of Pepper's Ghost creates stunning floating holograms visible from multiple angles without special glasses.

Multi-Layer Projection

Stack multiple transparent layers to create complex 3D scenes with incredible depth and detail.

Interactive Sensing

Built-in sensors detect hand gestures, eye tracking, and proximity for natural interaction with holographic content.

Modular Design

Scalable system that can be configured for small displays or large installation walls.

Real-World Applications

From intimate home installations to large-scale museum experiences, Glasscape adapts to any environment.
